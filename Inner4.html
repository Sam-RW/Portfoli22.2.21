  • Mechatronics

  • See Full Report
  • This project goal was to design a mechatronic system to locate magnets and mark them with plastic cups within an XY gantry. The solution involved a linear scanning system with three hall effect sensors and a helical screw cup dropping mechanism. My role in this project was the design and complete construction of the mechanical system and software implementation.
